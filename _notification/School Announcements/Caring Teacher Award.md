# **Caring Teacher Award (CTA) 2026**

![](/images/Announcement/2025/CTA_Poster_2025.jpg)

Organised by National Institute of Education (NIE), this biennial award aims to acknowledge teachers who demonstrate exemplary care and concern for the academic, moral, social, emotional, and mental well-being of their students. 

Take time to show your appreciation to the teachers of your child/ward by nominating them for the Caring Teacher Awards (CTA) 2026. The closing date is **31 December 2025**.

Click on the link below to submit your nomination online:
[https://www.cta.nie.edu.sg/nominate](https://www.cta.nie.edu.sg/nominate)

More details can also be found on the CTA website ([www.cta.nie.edu.sg](www.cta.nie.edu.sg)).
